Who We Are

We’re a community-driven initiative proudly run by a passionate committee of local farmers and small business owners. Together, we’ve created a space where fresh, seasonal produce meets handcrafted goods, and neighbors gather to celebrate the beauty of buying local.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to connect our community with the freshest, locally-grown food & thoughtfully crafted goods while fostering relationships between neighbors, farmers, and artisans.
