About Me!

With joy in all I do!

I bring together research, design, and entrepreneurship to explore how technology can solve meaningful problems. My experiences span AI safety testing, healthcare research, and strategic consulting, all grounded in curiosity and a drive to create impact. At the core, I aim to forge genuine human impact by building real bonds and connections that make innovation matter beyond the lab or boardroom.

AN IMPERATIVE TIME TO CARE ABOUT AI.

Pilot Study

This summer, I led a pilot study at Boston Children’s Hospital and Harvard Medical School examining how large language models communicate information about Alzheimer’s drugs. The project revealed critical insights into the risks of misinformation and the opportunities for AI to support public health literacy.

Publication in Progress

Building on ethical AI use in healthcare, I authored a paper exploring how JIA patients and their caregivers perceive the use of AI models in evaluating treatment and providing diagnoses. Their voices underscored the importance of trust, empathy, and transparency in the design of healthcare technologies.
