Grandma’s Homemade Pie (Apple)

Recipe by Nicholas Morgenstern

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt · 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup cold butter (cubed)
  • 5–7 tbsp ice water
  • 6 cups sliced apples
  • ¾ cup sugar · 2 tsp cinnamon

Notes by me

Photos by Eva Chaston

Method

  1. Mix flour, salt, and sugar. Cut in butter until pea-sized.
  2. Add ice water just until dough holds together. Chill 30 min.
  3. Roll out, line pan, fill with apples + cinnamon sugar.
  4. Cover with lattice, brush with egg, sprinkle sugar.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45–55 min. Cool 2 hrs. Serve warm.

Blueberry Scones

From the kitchen of Ash

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour · 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda · ½ tsp salt
  • ⅓ cup sugar · zest of 1 lemon
  • ½ cup cold butter · ¾ cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup blueberries
Tip: freeze berries so they don’t burst.

Method

  1. Whisk dry ingredients; rub in butter.
  2. Stir in buttermilk, then fold berries. Pat to disk.
  3. Cut into 8 wedges, brush cream, sprinkle sugar.
  4. Bake 400°F (205°C) for 18–22 min. Cool slightly, devour.
