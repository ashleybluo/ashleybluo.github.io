Chapter 1

As I Understand It

Life is beautifully strung together by small moments. The people I love have shaped me in ways I’ll never fully be able to name. Their presence, their trust, their chaos and clarity has molded me into who I’ve become. I carry that with me always, and to them, I am forever indebted. Even in the presence of fear and the daunting possibility of failure, I have come to derive my joy from the journey. As corny as this all sounds, that is to say: living is so lovely and I carry this outlook with me wherever I go.
